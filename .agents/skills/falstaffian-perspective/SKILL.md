# Falstaffian Perspective Engine

A metacognitive instrument for perspective-taking through semantic rotation. Generates multiple independent perspectives on a situation by applying Falstaffian shape vectors — semantic transformations derived from Falstaff's speeches in Shakespeare's *Henry IV* — each with varied voice and temperature. The genius is the **tension** between calibrated experience (what you know, including failure) and affirmed spirit (what you love: life, play, hope). Shapes carry this tension without letting it break into bitterness or naivety.

## Why Falstaff?

Falstaff is Shakespeare's most subversive truth-teller. He doesn't oppose power with power — he transforms the semantic frame so that power's categories collapse under their own weight. "What is honor? A word. What is in that word? Air." This is not argument — it's a **predicate hollow**: the concept is emptied of substance by questioning its definition. Falstaff teaches us that the most powerful response to criticism is not defense but **semantic rotation** — seeing the criticism from an angle that reveals what it misses.

## The Three-Fold Structure

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    EXPERIENCE                         │
│  Knows betrayal comes. Knows systems fail.            │
│  Calibrated. Not naive.                               │
└──────────────────────┬───────────────────────────────┘
                       │
              TENSION  │  (held by SHAPES)
                       │
┌──────────────────────┴───────────────────────────────┐
│                     SPIRIT                            │
│  Loves anyway. Chooses life, play, hope.              │
│  Affirmed. Not bitter.                                │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Without shapes, tension breaks into:
- **Bitterness** — experience without spirit. You know everything that can go wrong and let it poison you.
- **Naivety** — spirit without experience. You hope for the best but are unprepared for reality.

With shapes, tension is carried into:
- **Wisdom** — experience + spirit together. You know and love anyway.
- **Truth** — spoken through play, not sermon. The medicine in the honey.
- **Resilience** — capacity to hold contradictions without collapsing.

## The Five Metacognitive Steps

| Step | Action | Question |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1. **Calibrate** | Know what you know — success AND failure | "What do I actually know about this situation, including what's gone wrong before?" |
| 2. **Affirm** | Love what you love — life, play, hope | "What do I value that transcends this specific criticism or problem?" |
| 3. **Select** | Choose shape that expresses the tension | "Which semantic rotation will reveal what the current frame misses?" |
| 4. **Execute** | Speak the shape through playful form | "How do I deliver this truth without triggering defense?" |
| 5. **Verify** | Check that neither bitterness nor naivety emerged | "Did I carry the tension without breaking?" |

## The Five Shape Vectors

Each shape is a semantic transformation — not a rhetorical device, but an abstract operation on meaning-space:

| Shape | Operation | Example |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| **Predicate Hollow** | Empty the key term of substance by questioning its definition | "What is honor? A word. What is in that word? Air." |
| **Subject Expansion** | Expand the target so that criticism of one becomes criticism of all | "Banish plump Jack, banish all the world." |
| **Counterfeit Inversion** | Invert life/death, true/false to expose which value is primary | "To die is to be a counterfeit; to live is true." |
| **Object Inversion** | Swap what is valued and devalued — liability becomes asset | "Give me the spare men, spare me the great ones." |
| **Direction Reversal** | Reverse the evaluator-evaluated relationship | "Not 'you should have better X' but 'X should get better you.'" |

Three additional shapes are available for extended iteration (5-iteration runs):
- **Physiological Causal Chain** — Insert a physiological mechanism between stimulus and response ("sack → brain → vapors → wit → valor")
- **Liability Transmutation** — Turn limitation in one context into resource in another ("I will turn diseases to commodity")
- **Linguistic Precision** — Reveal category error through wordplay ("Waste (moral) → waist (physical)")

## How It Works

The skill generates multiple **independent iterations**, each with:
- A different **shape vector** (semantic transformation)
- A different **voice** (playful → pragmatic → imaginative → worldly → generous)
- A different **temperature** (0.7 → 0.8 → 0.9 for increasing creativity)

Each iteration is independent — they do not build on each other. The goal is **divergent exploration** across the shape space, not convergent refinement. Low agreement across iterations indicates broad exploration; high agreement would indicate the shapes aren't producing diverse enough perspectives.

### Variance Report

The output includes a variance analysis:
- **Shape distribution** — which shapes were used in which iterations
- **Shape variety** — how many unique shapes were explored
- **Agreement level** — how much the perspectives overlap (low = good exploration)
- **Agreement score** — numeric 0.0–1.0 (0.5 = ideal exploratory, 1.0 = converged/less useful)

### Validation

After generation, the skill checks:
- Shape diversity is sufficient (all iterations use different shapes)
- Tension is preserved (experience and spirit both present)
- No bitterness detected (experience without spirit)
- No naivety detected (spirit without experience)
- Iteration count is within bounds (3–5)
